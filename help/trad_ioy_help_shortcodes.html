Right now Traditores In-One-Year supports a single shortcode, tradioy. It has two attributes:

  • type — all66 to read the whole Bible in one year or nt for just the New Testament. (More versions to follow upon request.)
  • scroll to override the configurations "Scroll to today's entry." Y, y;, or 1; will be considered yes, everything else no.

Examples

To insert a table that has a whole Bible reading plan:

[tradioy type="all66"]

To insert a table that has a New Testament only reading plan that turns on the scrolling behavior (regardless of what is set in the configuration):

[tradioy type="nt" scroll="y"]
